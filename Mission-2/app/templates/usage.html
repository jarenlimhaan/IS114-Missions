How to use SHAS?
Using the Smart Home Alarm System (SHAS) is designed to be intuitive and engaging, ensuring a smoother wake-up experience.
To get started, users simply set their desired wake-up time through the app:
  1. When the alarm goes off, it will initiate one of the built-in gamesâ€”a flappy bird or a maze challenge.
  2. To turn off the alarm, users must complete the game, which encourages full engagement and helps shake off sleepiness.
  3. As users play, a built-in timer records the duration between the alarm sounding and the completion of the game, providing valuable insights into their waking habits.
Users can review this data daily to identify patterns and make necessary adjustments to their sleep routine. With SHAS, waking up on time becomes a fun and interactive experience, ultimately promoting healthier sleep habits.
