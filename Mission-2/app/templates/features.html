  • "Personalised Alarm"

    The SHAS alarm ensures users wake up at their scheduled time by persisting until they complete an interactive game. Traditional snoozing isn't an option, making it a reliable tool for cultivating consistent wake-up routines.

  • "Flappy Bird"

    This mini-game requires players to tap and navigate a bird through a series of obstacles. Its challenging yet engaging mechanics ensure the user is fully alert before the alarm can be silenced.

  • "The Maze"

    The maze game demands focus and problem-solving as users guide a character through a complex labyrinth. This ensures mental engagement, helping users transition smoothly from sleep to wakefulness.

  • "Accelerometer Feature"

    The SHAS device integrates an accelerometer to detect movement, ensuring the user is physically active when turning off the alarm. This feature prevents users from simply silencing the alarm while lying down, promoting complete wakefulness by requiring intentional physical effort, such as picking up the device or tilting it in a specific direction.

  • "Tracking Routine"

    SHAS includes a data logging feature that tracks the time taken to turn off the alarm, from the initial alarm sound to the completion of the game. This data allows users to monitor and adjust their wake-up habits for long-term sleep improvement.
